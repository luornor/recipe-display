Tofu Curry

Tofu Curry Recipe by Sanchez
25 mins
Ingredients:
  • 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread for serving
Instructions:
  1. STEP 1

    Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside.

  2. STEP 2

    In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. STEP 3

    Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan, and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  4. STEP 4

    Stir in the diced bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, cauliflower florets, and broccoli florets. Cook for 5-6 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

  5. STEP 5

    In a small bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, red curry paste, soy sauce, and brown sugar (if using) until well combined.

  6. STEP 6

    Pour the coconut milk mixture into the pan with the vegetables. Stir to combine.

  7. STEP 7

    Return the cooked tofu to the pan and gently stir to coat it with the curry sauce.

  8. STEP 8

    Bring the curry to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are cooked to your liking and the sauce has thickened.

  9. STEP 9

    Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the pan from heat and garnish the tofu curry with fresh cilantro.

  10. STEP 10

    Serve hot over cooked rice or with naan bread on the side. Enjoy your delicious Tofu Curry!
