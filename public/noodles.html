Veg Noodles

Veg Noodles Recipe by Carlos
25 mins
Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200g noodles (egg or rice noodles)
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish (optional)
Instructions:
  1. STEP 1

    Cook the noodles according to package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  2. STEP 2

    Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and sliced onion, and cook until fragrant and onion is translucent, about 2 minutes.

  3. STEP 3

    Add julienned carrots, sliced bell pepper, broccoli florets, and snap peas to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. STEP 4

    Add the cooked noodles to the pan along with soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Toss everything together until the noodles and vegetables are well coated with the sauce.

  5. STEP 5

    Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning and sauce according to your preference.

  6. STEP 6

    Remove from heat and transfer the vegetable noodles to serving plates.

  7. STEP 7

    Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired. Serve hot and enjoy Serve your delicious Vegetable Noodles!
